Previous Relationships

Before dating Martinez, Allan had been married and divorced three times. Tracy Taylor, whom he married in 1987, was his first wife. Maggie Herzberg, Tanna Herzberg, and Dallas Herzberg are the names of the couple’s three daughters.

After divorcing Taylor, he married Danette Day on November 28, 1998, and divorced her seven months later in June 1999. On June 5, 2001, he married Angela. Angela, his third wife, tragically committed suicide on October 25, 2004.
