Galarian Darmanitan Theme Deck

Box art
Release dateAugust 14, 2020

The Galarian Darmanitan Theme Deck from the Darkness Ablaze expansion of the Pokémon Trading Card Game focuses on Pokémon.

Like other Theme Decks, the Galarian Darmanitan Theme Deck includes damage counters, a one-player playmat, a Galarian Darmanitan deckbox, a custom coin, and a TCGO code card. The Galarian Darmanitan Theme Deck also includes a Quick Start Rules booklet to help players new to the game.

Description

Cold as the north wind—or blazing with fiery determination—Galarian Darmanitan shows off deep composure and immense strength in battle! Professor's Research keeps your hand full of options, and Pelipper keeps an eye out from the sky. Suicune, Kangaskhan, and Beartic add strong offense to keep the Galarian Darmanitan theme deck extra frosty!

Trivia

  • The Cracked Ice Holofoil version of Galarian Darmanitan is exclusive to this deck.
  • The Non Holofoil version of Suicune is exclusive to this deck and the Inteleon V League Battle Deck.
  • The Non Holofoil version of Kangaskhan is exclusive to this deck and the Darkness Ablaze Build & Battle Box.
  • The Non Holofoil version of Professor's Research was previously included in the Sword & Shield Build & Battle Box and Rillaboom, Cinderace, and Inteleon Theme Decks.
  • The TCGO deck unlocked with the code card provided differed slightly to the physical deck. Instead of the "Sonia" and "Capacious Bucket" copies, the deck contained two copies of "Lucky Egg" and "Air Balloon" instead.
