Drake Wuertz Was Extremely Offended By CM Punks Pro-Choice T-Shirt On AEW Dynamite

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Drake Wuertz was ostracized from mainstream pro wrestling after continually voicing support for the debunked QAnon conspiracy cult. Wuertz has since started campaign to become a state legislature in Florida’s 30th District. Wuertz recently took great offense at what he believed was a walking advertisement for murder by CM Punk.

On last night’s episode of AEW Dynamite, Punk was on guest commentary for the bout between AEW World Champion Adam Page and Konosuke Takeshita. Punk was sporting a shirt with a pro-life message when he made his entrance.

Drake Wuertz took great offense to Punk’s stance in favor of reproductive health. Drake and other QAnon followers take anti-abortion activism to the extreme, often subscribing to the theory that a left-wing cabal of power brokers sexually violates, kills, and drinks the blood of babies to gain their essence. Drake was not a fan of Punk’s political statement, voicing his displeasure on social media.

“Liberal Athiest CM Punk promoting child sacrifice on a live Pro Wrestling show is disgraceful.

There is no “Right” to murder a baby.”

Drake Wuertz has repeatedly said that abortion is the biggest reason he is running for a legislature seat. Wuertz refers to the topic of abortion as a “medical freedom” issue. He also lumps anti-vaccination beliefs and other anti-public safety stances into the same basket.

The issue has been hotly debated recently as a leaked Supreme Court draft decision hints that the nation’s highest court intends to overturn the long-established federal right to an abortion. As a legislator, Drake Wuertz would endeavor to ban the practice completely in Florida. CM Punk believes in a woman’s right to choose.
