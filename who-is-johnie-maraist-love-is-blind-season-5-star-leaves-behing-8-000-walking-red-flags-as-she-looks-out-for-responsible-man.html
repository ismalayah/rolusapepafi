'Love is Blind' Season 5 to bring heartbreaks, love triangles, and happily ever after

'Love is Blind' will once again show how different can love be. Some may end up heartbroken, some may discover their happily ever after, and others might find themselves in complicated situations.

The trailer hints there will be dating insecurities, post-pod proposal drama, family feuds over the love decisions made, and a potential love triangle.

In the trailer for 'Love is Blind' Season 5, two men say, "She planned for us to both be here because she couldn’t let me go," hinting at a love triangle.

The official synopsis claims, "Singles who want to be loved for who they are have signed up for a less conventional approach to modern dating in Houston, and will choose someone to marry without ever meeting them."

It added, "Over several weeks, the newly engaged couples will move in together, plan their wedding and find out if their physical connection matches their strong emotional bond developed in the Pods."

"When their wedding day arrives, will real-world realities and external factors push them apart, or will they marry the person they fell blindly in love with? Hosted by Nick and Vanessa Lachey," it further claimed.

'Love is Blind' Season 5 will be released on September 22 on Netflix.
